Privacy Policy Privacy Policy — Huit.AI / HyperLoanAI
+

Effective Date: March 2026  |  Entity: Huit.AI  |  Contact: privacy@huit.ai

1. Information We Collect

We collect: account information (name, email, company, NMLS ID); usage data (platform interactions, feature usage, log data); content you provide (contact lists, pipeline records, documents you upload); and payment information processed by Stripe (Huit.AI does not store payment card data).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to: deliver and operate the Huit.AI platform; provide customer support; communicate platform updates; comply with legal obligations; and detect and prevent fraud or abuse. We do not sell your personal information.

3. Data Sharing

We share data only with service providers necessary to deliver the platform: Supabase (database hosting), Stripe (payment processing — PCI DSS compliant), Vercel (application hosting), OpenAI API (AI inference). A complete subprocessor list is available upon request at privacy@huit.ai.

4. AI Model Training

Your data will never be used to train, fine-tune, or develop any Huit.AI AI model without your explicit written consent. All AI model inputs are derived exclusively from publicly available CFPB HMDA LAR data and NMLS public records.

5. Data Security

We maintain a written information security program compliant with the GLBA Safeguards Rule (16 C.F.R. Part 314). Controls include encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest, role-based access controls, audit logging, and annual security review. Breach notification within 72 hours of discovery.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Account data is retained while your subscription is active. Upon termination: 30-day export window; data deleted within 90 days; written deletion confirmation provided upon request. Submit deletion requests to privacy@huit.ai.

7. California Residents — CCPA Rights

California residents have the right to: (a) know what personal information we collect; (b) request deletion of personal information; (c) opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data); and (d) non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights. Submit CCPA requests to privacy@huit.ai.

8. GLBA Notice

As a technology service provider to regulated financial institutions, Huit.AI operates as a service provider under the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act. We do not collect or use nonpublic personal information from financial institution customers except as necessary to deliver contracted services.

APEX / FCRA APEX Permissible Use Notice & FCRA Disclosure
+

Applicable to: All APEX and REAPEX module subscribers  |  Contact: compliance@huit.ai

Important: By activating APEX, subscribers make binding legal representations regarding their intended use. Read this notice in full before activating the APEX module.

1. What APEX Is

APEX is Huit.AI's talent intelligence platform. It identifies and scores mortgage loan officers using the Transition Likelihood Score (TLS) and Source Talent Detection Algorithm (STDA), derived exclusively from publicly available CFPB HMDA LAR data (2017–2024) and NMLS public licensee records. APEX does not use credit bureau data, non-public personal financial information, or any individual's private records.

2. FCRA Analysis — Authorized Use

Huit.AI has conducted a full Fair Credit Reporting Act (15 U.S.C. §1681) applicability analysis. Conclusion: TLS scores and APEX intelligence do not constitute consumer reports when used solely for recruiting outreach prioritization. APEX output is authorized for: (a) identifying loan officers for recruiting outreach; (b) retention planning for existing team members; (c) mortgage market intelligence and landscape analysis.

3. Prohibited Uses

  • No adverse employment decisions: APEX output may NOT be used as the basis for termination, demotion, reassignment, denial of promotion, or any other adverse employment action.
  • No employment screening: APEX output may NOT be shared with any third party for employment screening purposes.
  • No credit decisions: APEX output may NOT be used to make or inform any credit, lending, or financial product decision about any individual.

4. Permissible Use Certification

All APEX subscribers must execute the Permissible Use Certification (v2.0) at account activation, certifying: (1) APEX used for outreach/retention only; (2) no adverse employment decisions; (3) no third-party sharing for employment purposes; (4) independent FCRA/NLRA/state employment law compliance; (5) legitimate business purpose for each list generated.

5. Data Provenance

All APEX model inputs are derived exclusively from: CFPB HMDA public LAR data (cfpb.gov) and NMLS public licensee records (nmlsconsumeraccess.org). No non-public data. No credit bureau data. No individually identifiable non-public financial information.

6. Loan Officer Rights

Licensed mortgage professionals whose public data appears in the APEX system have the following rights: right to know (compliance@huit.ai), right to correct factual inaccuracies, right to opt out of APEX outreach lists. Contact compliance@huit.ai with NMLS license number to exercise any right.

7. NLRA Section 7 Notice

Employers using APEX to monitor their own current employees should consult labor counsel regarding NLRA Section 7 obligations before deploying APEX for internal retention surveillance. Huit.AI is a data intelligence vendor — not your legal counsel.

8. Model Validation

TLS and Lead Scoring Engine third-party validation is in-flight and expected to be complete prior to enterprise (COMMAND/DOMINATE tier) client deployments. FFIEC SR 11-7 model documentation available to enterprise clients upon request at compliance@huit.ai.

Questions about APEX permissible use or FCRA compliance: compliance@huit.ai
